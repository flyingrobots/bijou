# DL-006 — Prove Inspector Panel Rhythm

Legend: [DL — Design Language](/Users/james/git/bijou/docs/legends/DL-design-language.md)

## Idea

DL-005 proves the guided-flow side of the original design-language follow-on through a canonical `explainability()` surface.

The inspector side is still waiting:

- side panels with current-value emphasis
- compact titled sections
- calm supporting-copy hierarchy
- one obvious relationship between the main task and the side context

## Why

Inspector panels remain one of Bijou's strongest future block candidates.

They sit between drawers, settings, and guided flows:

- more supplemental than a modal
- more structured than a text blob
- calmer than a dense debug pane

If the design language is going to produce canonical blocks, inspector-panel rhythm needs its own proof cycle instead of hiding inside one-off examples.
