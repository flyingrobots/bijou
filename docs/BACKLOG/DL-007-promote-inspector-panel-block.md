# DL-007 — Promote Inspector Panel Block

Legend: [DL — Design Language](/Users/james/git/bijou/docs/legends/DL-design-language.md)

## Idea

DL-006 proves the first canonical inspector seam through `inspector()` and the DOGFOOD variants pane.

The next step should ask whether inspector language is ready to graduate from one reusable component to a fuller block candidate.

That likely means proving inspector reuse in another product surface and deciding:

- what belongs in the core component
- what belongs in a future block/runtime
- what interaction ownership an inspector block should have

## Why

Inspector panels are one of the clearest future block candidates in the design-language docs.

Promoting them too early would freeze the wrong shape.

Never promoting them would trap the good work inside one side panel and one component.
