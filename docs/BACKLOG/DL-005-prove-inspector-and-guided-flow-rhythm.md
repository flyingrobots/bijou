# DL-005 — Prove Inspector and Guided Flow Rhythm

Legend: [DL — Design Language](/Users/james/git/bijou/docs/legends/DL-design-language.md)

## Idea

DL-004 proves calmer drawer rhythm and structured notice rows in the shell notification center.

The next proof slice should move into other likely block candidates:

- inspector-style side panels
- guided flows and explainability walkthroughs
- section rhythm for compact review/edit surfaces

## Why

These surfaces sit directly on the path toward canonical blocks.

If the design language is going to hold outside lists, palettes, and notices, it needs to keep proving itself in multi-section review and guidance surfaces before we promote those structures into reusable blocks.

Backlog spawned by the retrospective for DL-004.
