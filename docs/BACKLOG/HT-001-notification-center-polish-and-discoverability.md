# HT-001 — Notification Center Polish and Discoverability

Legend: [HT — Humane Terminal](/Users/james/git/bijou/docs/legends/HT-humane-terminal.md)

## Idea

The shell-owned notification center exists, but it still needs product-level polish:

- clearer discoverability
- calmer footer cues
- better review/history ergonomics
- more intentional interaction treatment

## Why

Notifications are part of the shell product, but they are not the next design-language cycle.

This work should stay explicitly on the backlog while doctrine is captured, then come back as a focused Humane Terminal cycle.

## Status

Parked on the backlog while DL-001 captures more design language.
